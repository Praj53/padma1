About Our

Manufacturing

Company

Step into the world of agricultural innovation with Padma Industries, a trailblazing company rooted in the visionary ideals of its founder, the late Shri. Dattatray V. Yadav. Established in 1989 in Umbraj, Maharashtra, India, Padma Industries embarked on a journey to revolutionize Indian agriculture through mechanization. What started as a humble endeavor focusing on spare parts, trolleys, and agricultural equipment, swiftly evolved into a comprehensive production line of top-notch agricultural implements, proudly bearing the brand name "Padma".
